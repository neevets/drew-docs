# Pricing

## Choose your protection level

Drew ships as premium by default, with a 5-day free tier so you can test the full suite before upgrading.

=== Standard Plan
Start with 5 days free, then keep the core stack.

`$3 / month`

- All premium protection, including a 5-day free tier
- Adaptive automod rules and filtered invites
- Anti-raid burst detection with lockdown toggles
- Instant rollback for channel and role nukes
- Community reports and moderation summaries
- Standard support response times

=== Advanced Plan
Maximum coverage with priority escalations.

`Most Popular`

`$7 / month`

- Unlimited automod presets and server profiles
- Priority anti-raid monitoring and live incident alerts
- Custom bot name and avatar branding
- Extended audit logs and exportable reports
- Priority support with faster response times

!!!primary Upgrade Path
After the 5-day free tier expires, an active premium subscription is required to continue using Drew.
